Final Project Proposal

Topic:

My website provides an introduction to the Phoenix Suns NBA team, including the current season (23-24 season) and the team's history.

Intended Audience:

Sports fans, basketball fans and those who want to know more about Phoenix Suns

Design & Style:

The website will primarily utilize warm colors to complement the Phoenix Suns team logo and uniform colors. The primary colors will be orange, followed by yellow, while blue and black can be used as background colors.

Links to three websites:

  • https://www.basketball-reference.com/
  • http://stats.nba.com
  • https://www.nba.com/suns/roster

Scope:

My website will consist of four sections. The first section will provide an overview of the current status of the Phoenix Suns, including details such as the roster of 2023-24 season. The second section will feature profiles of legends in the team's history. Next will be a recap of the significant seasons in the team's history, specifically focusing on postseason histories. Finally, there will be an image gallery.

Extras:

JSONP API and Event-driven DOM Manipulation
